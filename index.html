Welcome to ETFsCanada.ca

Navigating the growing world of ETFs in Canada can be complex. This independent resource helps you discover, understand, and compare the options available, empowering you to make informed investment decisions aligned with your goals.

From simple all-in-one solutions to specific sector, region, or thematic exposures, we provide clear comparisons of fees (MER), holdings characteristics, structure, performance (as of reported date), and more.

For a broader guide on DIY investing in Canada, check out our companion site: www.icaninvest.ca.

Site Status: This site is under active development! More ETF categories and funds are being researched and added regularly. We aim to build the best free resource for comparing Canadian ETFs.

Feedback & Feature Requests are highly encouraged! Let us know what you'd find valuable via the contact email below. Future ideas include advanced filtering, dividend tracking, and more, depending on interest.
